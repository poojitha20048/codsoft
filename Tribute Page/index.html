About Bill Gates

Bill Gates is an American business magnate, software developer, and philanthropist. As the co-founder of Microsoft, he revolutionized personal computing. Today, through the Bill & Melinda Gates Foundation, he works to improve global health, reduce poverty, and expand access to education across the world.

Milestones

2000 - Establishes the Bill & Melinda Gates Foundation

Why I Admire Him

Bill Gates is admired for his vision, resilience, and deep commitment to making a difference. Not only did he help shape the digital era, but he has also dedicated billions of dollars towards fighting disease and promoting education globally. His legacy inspires countless entrepreneurs and changemakers to strive for a better world.
